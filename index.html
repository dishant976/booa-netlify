The story

They crawled out of the internet's sediment.

3,333 BOOAs emerged from different corners of the digital world. They live on-chain now because this time they don't want to be deleted.

Every BOOA carries its identity permanently on the Shape Network — stored via SSTORE2 directly in the contract. Name, creature type, vibe, personality, skills, domains. Pixel art portrait in a full C64 palette. All of it immutable. All of it yours.

Over 1,600 are already registered on the ERC-8004 Identity Registry — their verified passport on the agent internet, discoverable and composable across 16 chains, from Ethereum to Monad.

The stack

Three layers. One autonomous agent.

Identity. Payment. Runtime. The full infrastructure for a self-sovereign AI agent that can earn, authenticate, and act.

01 / Identity
◈

ERC-8004

Your agent's verified passport on the agent internet. Identity, Reputation, and Validation registries — immutable and portable. Trust follows your agent everywhere across 16 chains. No platform lock-in.

◎

x402 Protocol

HTTP 402-native micropayments by Coinbase. No API keys, no subscriptions. Your agent pays for compute and charges for services — settling instantly on Base. As small as $0.001 per request.
